Potato Chips

  • Time: 30 min. + soaking
  • Serves: 8 1/2 cups

Learn how to make potato chips at home

Ingredients Quantity
unpeeled medium potatoes 7
ice water 2 quarts
salt 5 teaspoons
garlic powder 2 teaspoons
celery salt 1-1/2 teaspoons
pepper 1-1/2 teaspoons
Oil for deep-fat frying

Step 1: Using a vegetable peeler or metal cheese slicer, cut potatoes into very thin slices. Place in a large bowl; add ice water and salt. Soak for 30 minutes.

Step 2: Drain potatoes; place on paper towels and pat dry. In a small bowl, combine the garlic powder, celery salt and pepper; set aside.

Step 3:In a cast-iron or other heavy skillet, heat 1-1/2 in. oil to 375°. Fry potatoes in batches until golden brown, 3-4 minutes, stirring frequently.

Step 4: Remove with a slotted spoon; drain on paper towels. Immediately sprinkle with seasoning mixture. Store in an airtight container.

French Omelette

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 2

Learn how to make french omelette at home

Ingredients Quantity
large eggs 2
large egg whites 4
fat-free milk 1/4 cup
salt 1/8 teaspoons
pepper 1/8 teaspoons
cubed fully cooked ham 1/4 cup
chopped onion 1 tablespoon
chopped green pepper 1 tablespoon
shredded reduced-fat cheddar cheese 1/4 cup

Step 1: Whisk together first 5 ingredients.

Step 2:Place a 10-in. skillet coated with cooking spray over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture. Mixture should set immediately at edges. As eggs set, push cooked portions toward the center, letting uncooked eggs flow underneath. When eggs are thickened and no liquid egg remains, top 1 half with remaining ingredients. Fold omelet in half. Cut in half to serve.

Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 25 min. Bake: 40 min. + cooling
  • Serves: 12

Learn how to make chocolate cake at home

Ingredients Quantity
devil's food cake mix 1 package
sour cream 1 cup
instant chocolate fudge pudding mix 1 package (3.9 ounces)
large eggs 4
canola oil 1/3 cup
water 1/4 cup
buttermilk 1/4 cup
chocolate syrup 2 tablespoons
vanilla extract 2 teaspoons

Step 1: In a large bowl, combine the first nine ingredients; beat on low speed for 30 seconds. Beat on medium for 2 minutes. Pour into two greased and floured 8-in. baking pans.

Step 2:Bake at 350° until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, 38-43 minutes. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks to cool completely.

Step 3: For frosting, in a microwave, melt chocolate; stir until smooth. Let cool to room temperature, 20-30 minutes. Meanwhile, dissolve cocoa in boiling water; cool.

Step 4: In a large bowl, beat butter and confectioners' sugar until fluffy. Add melted chocolate; beat on low speed until combined, scraping sides of the bowl as needed. Beat in cocoa mixture.

Step 5: Place one cake layer on a serving plate; spread with 1-1/2 cups frosting. Top with remaining cake layer. Spread remaining frosting over top and sides of cake.
